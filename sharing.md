### LinkedIn-length

We keep asking: how do we govern AI? Better regulation, better alignment research, better incentives. But what if the real obstacle is deeper?

Our new white paper "In Tech We Trust" argues that modern culture relates to technology the way previous ages related to god — with trust, reverence, and submission. The myth of progress, the worship of technologists, the faith that more tech always means a better world: these aren't just bad habits. They're features of a cultural paradigm that makes wise governance structurally impossible.

Join authors Rufus Pollock, Rosie Bell & Sylvie Barbier alongside invited experts for a live roundtable discussion tomorrow. [+ link]
